Our Story

A glimpse of our Journey

Our Beginning: The mission called Ecarma was was born in a moment of despair and awakening—in a stuck elevator. Even as Prashant waited to be bailed out of his claustrophobic jail between floors, the writing was clearly on the cobwebbed wall that stared him. “There is no escaping the pains besieging my small world of 40 apartments unless I could find an expert to prevent and respond to them promptly.” When after 20 excruciating minutes in the cage, Prashant was finally pulled out, Ecarma was born…delivered as an answer to the prayers of countless residents of lesser known apartment complexes who suffer the untold pains of poor maintenance silently every day of their lives.

Our Experience

Explainer Video

Welcome to Ecarma, maintain your common areas and homes painlessly, and rest assured: everything that can be solved will be solved.Ecarma is an organisation of techies and facility management experts with years of professional experience in property maintenance. Our leadership team, backed by a network of highly skilled technicians, is dedicated to providing comprehensive and cost-effective maintenance solutions for both homes and common areas. The tech-enabled services of Ecarma are based on a robust process designed to prevent problems and fix them promptly and effectively if they do occur. Since our inception in 2014, we have liberated hundreds of residents in several apartment complexes from their maintenance headaches. Homes in Ecarma managed societies may have their problems. But thankfully maintenance is not one of them

What We Serve

MAINTENANCE AUDIT

We audit every utility and service in an apartment that needs maintenance and results are logged scientifically, with list of issues and preferred solutions.

PREVENTIVE MAINTENANCE

With its vast experience in facility management and technology, Ecarma has designed a failsafe process that diagnoses problems before they show up!

RESPONSIVE REDRESSAL

Despite preventive maintenance, if and when problems surface, Ecarma’s technicians are always at hand to respond effectively and fast.

VENDOR MANAGEMENT

Ecarma has a network of tested and trusted vendors for both planned maintenance and dealing with acute issues.
